Unity C# Game Dev School

baldorf is a video game developed during the final year of my undergraduate degree for my senior design project. It was a great opportunity to work with a team of 5 other developers, project management and a sponsor - Mystic Rhoads Productions.

Overview

  1. Assigned to baldorf project and Mystic Rhoads Productions sponsor.
  2. Met with the sponsored company and relevanet documentation to gather inital requirements.
  3. The team initiated these requirements using an AGILE scrum development process following a roadmap containing Milestones, Epics, Storeis and Issues.
  4. Multiple weekly team meeting were continued throughout the year to develop proposed features found in the roadmap and sponsor feedback.
  5. Held weekly sponsor meetings with the sponsored company to provide a weekly status report and provide beneficial feedback for development.

Development

Development of the game was created in the Unity Game Engine mostly using the C# programming language. A total of 6 Releases were made were each platoform were updated, while utilizing a 2 week sprint cycle for the development.
