Description

Following a 2 month lesson plan, I was an instructor for primary-secondary school children covering the fundamentals and basics of coding. This was an after-school activity for the students, which allowed me to speak to a class of students ranging from 1st to 6th grade over this time.

The initial classes covered the basics of what coding is and its applications, focusing on the basics of the C programming language. The following courses touched on more in depth knowledge such as methods and classes. The final class ended with a breif Q&A of the industry and majoring in Computer Science as a whole, where the students ended within a hand-on activity covering what they learned in the problem-solving relam.

Achievements

I am very fortunate to be given the ability to teach what I love in my studies and hobbies. This experience solidified my abilities to teach complex topics in a simplified form and be much more patient and understanding with others. Some notable take-aways from this experience:

  • Taught a group of 10+ students over a course of three weeks, concluding with a hands-on activity
