Description

From January to April of 2022 I took part in the qaulity assurance of the video game 'Grim Tranquility' developed by Poorly Timed Games. Over the cycle, I was given developer mods to identify any shortcoming, bugs, or inconsistencies brought from the game. During this time I thoroughly tested the game for said bugs, as well as writing documentation and a review for the overall architecture of the game.

Grim Tranquility is available now on Steam where it is currently in early access.

Achievements

This was a phenominal opportunity for me to be apart of another game in development, and understand the overall thought-process of another team when creating new features. Below is a brief list of my overall role in the late stages of the development process.

  • Tested new video game for bugs at pre-production as a final testing phase
  • Used creative methods to identify bugs and wrote report within the allotted deadline
